Pot Pie

Description

You won't need fancy ingredients for this rustic recipe!

Give it a try, even if you don't like fish!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of a squash
  • 1-2 puff pastry sheets
  • 1 can of herring

Steps

  1. Prepare the squash and other ingredients
  2. Make a bechamel sauce and open the herring
  3. Use the puff pastry to start assembling, bake, and enjoy!
