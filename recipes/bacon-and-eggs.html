Bacon and Eggs

Description

Just like Calcifer, you can be a part of the cooking process!

Easy and straightforward!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of bacon
  • 12 large eggs
  • Other mix-ins

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon and other mix-ins
  2. Beat the eggs and whisk in other ingredients
  3. Pour the mixture of eggs, bacon, and other ingredients, and bake all together!
