Ramen

Description

This recipe will warm your soul!

Recall the scene in Ponyo when Sosuke and Ponyo were eating yummy ramen noodles prepared by Lisa!

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 package of ramen
  • Other seasonings of your choice!

Steps

  1. Combine the broth and noodles
  2. Bring to a boil, then add your other seasonings
  3. Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes, and then enjoy!
